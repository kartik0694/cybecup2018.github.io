Let's Know It...

Talkitout is a tantalizing trio of competitive events for all those willing to indulge in war of words.

Round 1

The first round aims at testing your ability to be heard in a crowd of very opinionated noises.What makes you better than the others? Show us in a Group Discussion.

Round 2

In this round show us your skills of persuasion in the form of debate. Cajole, contradict and carve your way to the final round

Round 3

Here in the Extempore round, prove your worth by simultaneously showcasing how well you think and speak and how fast you do it.

Excel this trinity and stand unparalleled at victory's summit.

The rules are as follows:
  1. All the necessary materials shall be provided on the spot.
  2. Unfriendly behaviour with event organisers will lead to disqualification.
  3. The decision by the judges shall be binding.
  4. Use of unfair means will lead to disqualification.

Judging Criteria:
  1. Quickness of response
  2. Knowledge of the subject
  3. Speaking skills
  4. Presentation
In this quest, words are your only aid.
Be wise, lest you should be lost in this literary cascade.
