Let's Know It...

For all the budding developers out there, here's a chance for showcasing your self created apps, games and websites to stand out in the crowd and thrive thoroughly into the fascinating world of code with CodeIT.


The rules are as follows:
  1. Participants have to build their apps ,site or game prehand only and they need to present it at the event.
  2. Maximum time for the presentation will be 5 minutes.
  3. Unfriendly behaviour with coordinators, volunteers or other teams would not be tolerated and would result in direct disqualification.
  4. The decision by the judges shall be considered as final one and can't be debated upon.
  5. Use of unfair means will lead to direct disqualification.

Judging Criteria:
  1. Ideas and Creativity
  2. Coding Skills
  3. The way participants present their ideas
